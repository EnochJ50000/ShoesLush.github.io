Features

  • Manage Categories and expenses

    Easily add categories and expenses according to your needs with our Categories feature.

  • Manage Amounts

    With our Amount feature, you can add the quantity and amount you need and you can also change the curency according to your country.

  • View Records

    You can view your past transactions in your records.

  • Delete records

    You can delete unnecessary records .

  • Set budget goals

    Keep track of your expenses by setting budget goals for each expense with our monthly or weekly options.
