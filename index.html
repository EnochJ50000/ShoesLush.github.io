YO CLASS

This project is about an e-learning application that initiates the effective communication between fellow students and lecturers via chatrooms and video conferencing tools

Member 1, Kibuuka Edwin
Member 2 , Barasa Elijah Maiga
Member 3, Ahmed
Member 4, Joel
Member 5, Mugisha Richard

Group connect app is an E-learning system developed to override the problems prevailing in the practicing manual system. No formal knowledge is needed for the user to use this system.

The application helps academically weak students get a chance to have extra office time with lecturers inorder to shape what they are lacking

This app also includes resources such as reading materials and reference books for those that cant download
